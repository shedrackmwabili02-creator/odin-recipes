Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Soft and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast. Serve with syrup, honey, or fresh fruits.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add eggs and milk, then mix well.
  3. Heat a pan and add butter.
  4. Pour batter and cook until golden brown.
  5. Flip and cook the other side.
