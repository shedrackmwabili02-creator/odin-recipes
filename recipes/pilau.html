Beef Pilau

Description

A rich and flavorful East African rice dish cooked with aromatic spices and tender beef. Perfect for special occasions and family meals.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 500g beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Pilau masala
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and pilau masala.
  3. Add beef and cook until tender.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add rice and water, then simmer until cooked.
